"A sound map of Bucharest" is a project that tries to map sounds of the city.
These are sounds that we might hear every day and not notice them,
but nevertheless they create interesting audioscapes and shape our environment and memories.

Recordings are made by my sound-geek friend Adriana Minu,
during our sound-mapping trip through Bucharest in June 2016.
